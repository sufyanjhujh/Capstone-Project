Institute of Emerging Career

At IEC Online, we equip adult learners to impact the world. We will guide, assist, and advocate for you in a Christ-centered atmosphere that focuses on program completion and academic excellence. Explore our associate, bachelor’s, master’s, and doctoral degrees and certificate programs designed to meet your needs as an adult learner. Plus students with previous college experience can seamlessly transfer to IEC Online with our generous transfer credit policies. Join the thousands of adult students that have earned accredited degrees from Colorado Christian University without ever setting foot on campus. Whether you routinely travel for work or have an irregular schedule, your CCU Online courses go with you. There are many benefits of earning a degree online, including taking courses at your own pace, having access to a community of learners just like you, and being able to find class resources at your fingertips.

PLUS

  • With a focus on servant leadership taught from a Christian worldview, our academics emphasize how personal values can impact your studies, life, and work.
  • You bring life experience to the table, so we have flexible transfer credit policies and ways to earn credit for prior life experiences.
  • Our cohort model allows you to get to know your fellow classmates in your online degree program whether they are cities, states, or countries away.
Our unique Hyflex learning model gives you the choices you need to balance your education and responsibilities. With Hyflex learning, you decide when to attend lectures in person or remotely. Do whatever works best for you, with the flexibility to choose a different option for each class session.
